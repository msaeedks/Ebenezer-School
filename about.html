Ebenezer English Medium UP School Thekkuthod Established Since 1960

Ebenezer EM UP School is located at a hill top with most beautiful view from above, the school is enriched with the nature beauty, it has many advanced infrastructures, the teacher student bond in this environment is very mutual..

A small river is flowing beside the school which feels very energetic and natural, students get a chance to study environment friendly.

The best Education is provided with qualified teachers and the smart class room which include the smart screen technology etc. Students get a chance to learn in an nature friendly school as the school is situated on the top of a hill. The infrastructures provided in the school is at the most. Students get extra curricular activities in their study syllubus to enrich their knowledge and boost up their brain.
